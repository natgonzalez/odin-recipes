Overnight Oats

Delicious overnight blueberry oats

Never had a taste for oatmeal? These overnight oats will give you a mouth-watering feel and having you wanting oats every single morning!

Made with cinnamon crunchy graham crackers, sweet honey and the milk of your choice, this is the healthiest breakfast alternative for anyone who doesn't like breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup rolled oats
  • 2/3 cup milk, of your choice
  • 1/2 cup vanilla greek yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1/3 graham cracker, crushed
  • Blueberries, optional
  • 1 teaspoon chia seed, optional

Steps

  1. In a mason jar or sealable container, add the oats, milk, yogurt, vanilla extract, chia seeds, cinnamon, blueberries, and graham crackers, and stir together.
  2. Seal and place in the refrigerator overnight for up to five days.
  3. Top with additional blueberries, if desired.
  4. Enjoy!
